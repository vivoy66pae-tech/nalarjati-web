# Why this site exists

I built **nalarjati.dev** as a permanent home for the kind of work I do every day: shipping AI features, modernizing infrastructure, and building web apps that don't fall over under load.

It's also a place to write down what I learn — short, opinionated, and free of fluff.

## What you can expect

- **AI engineering** — patterns for RAG, agents, and LLM integrations that survive contact with real users
- **DevOps notes** — the things that matter when you're on call at 2am
- **Web development** — Next.js, Node, performance, and the boring fundamentals

## How to follow along

The [RSS feed](/feed.xml) is the most reliable way. There's also a [newsletter](#newsletter) if you prefer email — no spam, unsubscribe with one click.

That's it. First post, short and sweet. More to come.
